The Cube house

Cube House is the final project for Architecture Design Studio for college freshmen.
The task is to design a weekend retreat for the university president, on a 80 by 80 foot piece of land on the shore of lake eerie, using hedges, a 20 by 20 foot cube, and 3 planes in the x, y and z axes.
The main focus points for the cube house are Spatial Definition, Spatial Relationships, Pragmaticism, Aesthetics and Overall sensibility as an architectural work. It is currently still in progres.
